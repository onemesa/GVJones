Our Mission

GV Jones & Associates, Inc. provides a full range of water and wastewater treatment process engineering services for private, municipal and industrial clients throughout Alaska and internationally. Our business philosophy is to remain current on treatment technology advances and regulatory requirements so we can offer our clients the best information possible as they plan and construct their treatment systems. If you are planning a new project or contemplating an upgrade, please contact us. Our small, efficient firm will work closely with you throughout the project.
